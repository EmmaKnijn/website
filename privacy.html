Here we make clear how we process your information and for what reason

1 Data we collect

1.1 Web services

We collect the following information when you access any of our sites except knijn.one or knijn.space

  • IP Address, in order to prevent abuse of our services
  • The timestamp of the request, in order to prevent abuse of our services
  • The page you visit, in order to discover faults in our services and prevent abuse.
  • The HTTP status code, in order to discover faults in our services.

This data is stored for 14 days, after which it is removed automatically.

This data is stored every request, as part of our webserver logs.

We collect the following information when you access knijn.one or knijn.space

  • Visit time, duration and country, in order to judge interest.
  • The page you visit, in order to discover faults in our services and judge interest.
  • The page you were sent from, in order to prevent abuse.
  • The browser, OS and device you visit from, in order to prevent abuse.

This data is stored anonymously and as aggregate data. It does not get deleted as it is not identifiable to someone.

This aggregate data may be shared publically.

2 Your rights in regards with your data

2.1 EEA Countries

If you live in a country that is part of the EEA, you have your rights as per the GDPR

2.2 Other countries

If you don't live in a country that is part of the EEA, you still have rights as per the GDPR on knijn.one services

2.3 Questions or comments

If you have any questions or comments, feel free to reach out to provacy@knijn.one
