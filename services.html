knijn.one knijn.space Public Services

I run a few services for the betterment of the internet

  • yuri.knijn.one - A SFW Yuri imageboard, feel free to upload some images!
  • z.knijn.one - Zipline, a file uploader and url shortener compatible with ShareX (requires account).
  • ntfy.knijn.one - A notification service.
  • umami.knijn.one - Anonymous analytics dashboard (requires account).
  • uptime.knijn.one/status/emma - Uptime monitering for my services
  • civmc-map.knijn.one - A map of the CivMC Minecraft server, with assets hosted on GitHub instead of behind DuckDNS

NOTE: Abuse will not be tolerated, and I reserve the right to terminate any service at any time if I deem it necessary.

I'm open to help out with hosting for small, cool or open source projects

I also provide a communal Mullvad account for people behind censorship, contact me if you are interested.

Some services require an account to use. If you'd like to make use of them, please contact me at accountrequest@knijn.one

For DMCA requests, please email me at dmca@knijn.one

For other service related questions or requests, please email me at webadmin@knijn.one
